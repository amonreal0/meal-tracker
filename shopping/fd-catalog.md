# FreshDirect catalog

Mapping of generic grocery item → the **exact FreshDirect product** we settled on. This is
what makes reordering fast and accurate: once an item is here, Claude goes straight to the
known product instead of searching and guessing.

How to use:
- When building a cart, check here **first** for each list item.
- When you pick a NEW product on FreshDirect, add a row here with its name + URL (or product
  ID) so next time is instant.
- If a chosen product was bad (wilted, wrong size, discontinued), note it and pick a new one.
