## Cook 1: Carne con papas en salsa verde

Use this as the week’s “home base” meal.

**Buy**
- 1 lb beef stew meat, pork shoulder chunks, or boneless chicken thighs  
- 2 medium Yukon gold potatoes  
- 1 jar salsa verde, or fresh tomatillos if you feel like it  
- 1 white onion  
- Cilantro  
- Corn tortillas  
- Small cabbage or pre-shredded cabbage  
- Limes  
- 1 avocado

**Fast method**
1. Sear meat if you have patience. Skip if not.
2. Instant Pot: meat + salsa verde + sliced onion + salt + a splash of water or broth.
3. Cook: chicken thighs ~10 min high pressure; beef/pork chunks ~30–35 min.
4. Add potato chunks after pressure release, then simmer/sauté until tender, or pressure cook 3–4 more min.
5. Eat with tortillas and cabbage-lime salad.

**Leftover move:** next day, reduce the sauce in a pan and make tacos with cabbage, cilantro, lime, and avocado.

# Recipe 1: Albóndigas in chipotle tomato broth

Makes **2 large servings**, maybe 3 if you add rice/tortillas.

## Ingredients

### Meatballs

- 1 lb ground beef, pork, or beef/pork mix
- ½ small onion, finely chopped or grated
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 egg
- ½ cup breadcrumbs
- ¼ cup chopped cilantro
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- ½ tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- ½ tsp dried oregano

### Chipotle tomato broth

- 1 tbsp olive oil or neutral oil
- ½ small onion, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 to 2 chipotles in adobo, chopped
- 1 tbsp adobo sauce from the can
- 1 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
- 1½ cups chicken broth
- ½ tsp salt, then adjust
- ½ tsp cumin
- ½ tsp dried oregano
- Lime juice, to finish
- Cilantro, to finish

### To serve

Choose one:

- Cooked white rice
- Tortillas
- Crusty bread

## Method

1. Mix all meatball ingredients in a bowl. Don’t overwork it.
2. Form into about 12 meatballs.
3. In a pot, heat oil. Sauté onion for 3–4 minutes.
4. Add garlic, chipotle, adobo, cumin, and oregano. Cook 30 seconds.
5. Add crushed tomatoes and chicken broth. Bring to a simmer.
6. Gently drop in the meatballs.
7. Cover and simmer 18–22 minutes, until cooked through.
8. Finish with lime and cilantro.

**Leftover move:** reheat and eat as tacos with cilantro, lime, and maybe avocado.

# Recipe 2: Harissa chicken thighs with couscous + cucumber salad

Makes **2 servings**.

## Ingredients

### Harissa chicken

- 1 to 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
- 2 tbsp harissa paste
- 1 tbsp Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon, half juiced and half saved for serving
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
- ¾ tsp kosher salt
- Black pepper

### Couscous

- ⅔ cup dry couscous
- ⅔ cup chicken broth or water
- 1 tsp olive oil
- Pinch of salt

### Cucumber tomato salad

- 3 Persian cucumbers, chopped
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- ¼ small onion, very thinly sliced
- ¼ cup parsley or cilantro, chopped
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt and pepper
- Optional: ¼ cup feta

### Yogurt sauce

- ½ cup Greek yogurt
- 1 small garlic clove, grated or minced
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Pinch of salt
- Optional: tiny spoon of harissa swirled in

## Method

1. Mix harissa, yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
2. Coat chicken with the marinade. Even 15 minutes is fine.
3. Cook chicken:
   - **Air fryer:** 390°F for about 14–18 minutes, flipping once.
   - **Skillet:** medium-high heat, 5–7 minutes per side.
4. Rest chicken for 5 minutes, then slice.
5. Make couscous: bring broth/water, oil, and salt to a boil. Stir in couscous, cover, turn off heat, wait 5 minutes, fluff.
6. Mix cucumber salad.
7. Mix yogurt sauce.
8. Serve chicken over couscous with salad and yogurt sauce.

# Recipe 3: Garlic chili noodles with ground pork

Makes **2 servings**.

## Ingredients

- 8 oz noodles
- ½ lb ground pork
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 3 scallions, sliced; separate white and green parts
- 2 tbsp chili crisp or chili oil
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp sesame oil, optional
- 1 tsp sugar, optional and not for sweetness — just balances the sauce; you can skip it
- ½ cup chicken broth or water
- 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 1 tbsp water
- Optional: 2 cups spinach
- Optional: cucumber slices on the side

## Method

1. Cook noodles. Save ½ cup noodle water, then drain.
2. Mix sauce: soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, chili crisp, broth/water, and cornstarch slurry.
3. In a pan, brown ground pork over medium-high heat.
4. Add garlic, ginger, and scallion whites. Cook 1 minute.
5. Add sauce and simmer until glossy, about 1–2 minutes.
6. Add noodles and toss. Add noodle water if needed.
7. Add spinach at the end if using.
8. Top with scallion greens and more chili crisp.

**Leftover move:** reheat in a pan with a splash of water. Add a fried egg if you want to make it feel new.
