# BLT with avocado and spicy mayo

A standout, near-no-cook dinner — the only "cooking" is crisping the bacon (skillet or air
fryer). Single-serve; scale up easily. Spicy mayo + avocado make it feel like a real dinner,
not a snack.

## Ingredients
- 3–4 slices bacon
- 2 slices good bread (sourdough or similar), toasted
- A few leaves lettuce (romaine/little gem) or a handful arugula
- 1 tomato, sliced
- ½–1 avocado, sliced
- Salt and pepper

### Spicy mayo
- 2 tbsp mayo *(on hand)*
- 1–2 tsp chili crisp *(on hand)* — or chipotle in adobo, mashed
- Squeeze of lemon (optional)

## Method
1. Cook bacon until crisp — skillet over medium, or air fryer ~8–10 min at 400°F.
2. Stir together the spicy mayo.
3. Toast bread. Spread spicy mayo on both slices.
4. Layer bacon, tomato (salt it), avocado, lettuce. Close, slice, eat.

**Notes:** salt the tomato slices so they taste like summer. Pickled jalapeños are a great add
if you want more heat/acid.
