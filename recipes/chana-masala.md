# Chana masala with naan

Makes **2 servings + leftovers**. Cheap, mostly pantry, leftover-friendly. Serve with warmed
naan and/or rice.

## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp neutral oil
- 1 yellow onion, finely diced
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated *(use the leftover ginger)*
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1½ tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp ground coriander (optional)
- ½ tsp turmeric (optional)
- ¼–½ tsp cayenne or chili powder, to taste
- 1 can (14–15 oz) diced or crushed tomatoes
- 2 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- ½ cup water
- ¾ tsp salt, then adjust
- Juice of ½ lemon, to finish
- Cilantro, to finish
- Naan and/or rice, to serve

## Method
1. Heat oil, sauté onion 5–6 min until soft and golden at edges.
2. Add garlic, ginger, and all spices; cook 30–60 sec until fragrant.
3. Add tomatoes; simmer 5 min, mashing a bit.
4. Add chickpeas, water, salt. Simmer 15–20 min until thickened.
5. Finish with lemon juice and cilantro. Adjust salt.
6. Serve with warmed naan and/or rice.

**Leftover move:** thickens overnight; loosen with a splash of water. Great with a fried egg
on top (once eggs are restocked).
