# Fish tacos with cabbage slaw, crema & lime

Makes **2 servings**. A ⭐ standout — crispy fish, crunchy lime slaw, a spicy crema, warm corn
tortillas. It's a "project" only in that there are a few components to set up like a little taco
bar; each part is simple. **Best eaten fresh** (the fish doesn't reheat crispy), so this is a
cook-and-eat night, not a leftovers night — though leftover fish + slaw make a good rice bowl.

## Ingredients

### Fish
- ~1 lb firm white fish fillets (cod, mahi, or tilapia), patted dry, cut into ~1-inch strips
- ⅓ cup flour (or cornstarch for extra-crisp/GF)
- 1 tsp ground cumin *(on hand)*, ½ tsp smoked paprika, ½ tsp garlic powder, salt + pepper
- 1 egg, beaten *(if breading — see method)*
- ¾ cup panko *(on hand, partial)* — for the breaded/air-fryer route
- Neutral oil, for the skillet route

### Cabbage slaw
- ~3 cups green cabbage, thinly shredded
- Juice of 1 lime + a pinch of salt
- 2 tbsp pickled jalapeños, chopped, + a splash of their brine *(on hand — your salad acid)*
- 1 tbsp olive oil *(on hand)*

### Lime crema
- ¼ cup Mexican crema or sour cream
- Juice of ½ lime, pinch of salt
- 1 tsp chipotle in adobo (mashed) or chili crisp *(on hand)* — for heat

### To serve
- 6–8 corn tortillas, warmed
- ½–1 avocado, sliced
- Lime wedges; extra pickled jalapeños

## Method
1. **Slaw:** toss cabbage with lime juice + salt and let sit 10 min so it softens and weeps;
   squeeze out excess liquid, then toss with jalapeños, brine, and olive oil. (Pre-salting keeps
   it crunchy, not watery — same trick as salads.)
2. **Crema:** stir crema + lime + salt + chipotle/chili crisp. Set aside.
3. **Bread the fish:** season fish. Either (a) **air-fryer/breaded:** dredge flour → egg → panko,
   air-fry ~10–12 min at 400°F, flipping once, until golden; or (b) **skillet:** dredge in
   seasoned flour/cornstarch only and shallow-fry in ~¼ inch oil ~2–3 min/side until crisp.
   Drain on a rack or paper towel; salt immediately.
4. **Warm tortillas** (dry skillet or open flame, ~30 sec/side). Double them up.
5. **Build:** crema on the tortilla → fish → slaw → avocado → extra jalapeños → lime squeeze.

**Leftover move:** leftover fish loses its crunch — turn it + slaw into a rice bowl with crema
and avocado the next day rather than re-tacoing.
