# Crispy tofu with chili-garlic sauce

Makes **2 servings**. Air-fryer crispy tofu in a glossy chili-garlic sauce over rice. Almost
entirely pantry staples already on hand — just buy the tofu (and rice/veg).

## Ingredients

### Tofu
- 1 block (14–16 oz) extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed
- 2 tbsp cornstarch *(on hand)*
- 1 tbsp neutral oil
- ¼ tsp salt

### Chili-garlic sauce
- 2 tbsp chili crisp *(on hand)*
- 1½ tbsp soy sauce *(on hand)*
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar *(on hand)*
- 1 tsp sesame oil *(on hand)*
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp ginger, grated *(leftover ginger)*
- 1 tsp sugar (optional, balances the sauce)
- 3 tbsp water

### To serve
- Cooked white rice
- Scallions, sliced *(leftover scallions)*
- Optional: steamed broccoli or any green

## Method
1. Toss cubed tofu with cornstarch, oil, and salt.
2. Air fry 400°F ~15–18 min, shaking once, until crisp.
3. Meanwhile, simmer all sauce ingredients in a pan 1–2 min until glossy.
4. Toss the crispy tofu in the sauce off heat (keep it crisp — don't over-simmer).
5. Serve over rice, topped with scallions. Steamed broccoli on the side if using.

**Leftover move:** reheat tofu in the air fryer (not microwave) to re-crisp; rice separate.
