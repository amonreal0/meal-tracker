# Italian chopped salad with salami, provolone, chickpeas

Makes **2 dinner-sized servings**. No-cook. Salami + provolone + greens overlap with the
week's sandwich backup so nothing goes to waste.

## Ingredients
- 1 box/bag arugula (or 1 romaine heart), chopped — ~6 cups
- 3–4 oz salami, diced
- 3–4 oz provolone, diced
- 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- ¼ red onion, thinly sliced
- Pepperoncini, sliced (optional)

### Dressing
- 3 tbsp olive oil *(on hand)*
- 1½ tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 tsp Dijon (optional)
- ½ tsp dried oregano *(on hand)*
- Salt and pepper

## Method
1. Whisk dressing.
2. Toss everything together. Let sit 5 min so the chickpeas/onion soak up dressing.

**Leftover move:** holds a few hours but dress to order if you can. Leftover salami/provolone
→ the no-cook sandwich backup.
