# Harissa chicken thighs with couscous + cucumber salad

Makes **2 servings**. Compact ingredient list — good for a delivery order.

## Ingredients

### Harissa chicken
- 1 to 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
- 2 tbsp harissa paste
- 1 tbsp Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon (half juiced, half saved for serving)
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
- ¾ tsp kosher salt
- Black pepper

### Couscous
- ⅔ cup dry couscous
- ⅔ cup chicken broth or water
- 1 tsp olive oil
- Pinch of salt

### Cucumber tomato salad
- 3 Persian cucumbers, chopped
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- ¼ small onion, very thinly sliced
- ¼ cup parsley or cilantro, chopped
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt and pepper
- Optional: ¼ cup feta

### Yogurt sauce
- ½ cup Greek yogurt
- 1 small garlic clove, grated
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Pinch of salt
- Optional: tiny spoon of harissa swirled in

## Method
1. Mix harissa, yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, paprika, salt, pepper.
2. Coat chicken with the marinade (even 15 min is fine).
3. Cook chicken:
   - **Air fryer:** 390°F, ~14–18 min, flip once.
   - **Skillet:** medium-high, 5–7 min per side.
4. Rest chicken 5 min, then slice.
5. Couscous: boil broth/water + oil + salt, stir in couscous, cover, off heat 5 min, fluff.
6. Mix cucumber salad. Mix yogurt sauce.
7. Serve chicken over couscous with salad and yogurt sauce.

**Leftover move:** salad bowl with leftover chicken, cucumber salad, greens, yogurt sauce.
